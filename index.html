    Description Games:

    Space Waves is an arcade game that challenges players to control an arrow and navigate through obstacles to reach the end of each level. The game features a total of 33 levels, and players have the freedom to choose any level they wish to play at any time. Each level presents a different difficulty, indicated by facial icons, allowing players to determine how daring they want to be. The primary objective across all 33 levels of Space Waves remains consistent: players must guide their arrow to the finish line without colliding with walls, rotating gears, or thorny obstacles. Each level is designed with its own unique level of challenge.
